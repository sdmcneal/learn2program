Learning HTML5 Drawing Canvas

Based on a tutorial at here

A canvas with the name myCanvas has been created for you.

  1. Draw Onto The Canvas With JavaScript: Draw a blue rectangle that is 100 pixels wide and 100 pixels tall starting at coordinate 50,50. Hint: Blue in hexadecimal is #0000FF.
  2. Paths: Draw 3 lines: from (100,200) to (300,200), from (200,200) to (200,400), and (100,400) to (300,400). What letter is this? Hint: Do all your moveTo's and lineTo's before you use stroke().
  3. Arc's and Circles: Draw a half circle starting at (300,100) with a radius of 50. Hint: The circumference of a full circle is 2*Pi. The circumference of a half-circle is Pi.
  4. Test: Draw the words Ian rocks! with no fill in 35px Arial font at location 100,300.
